Goldfish

My pets name is Goldy, he is a goldfish. I got my first goldfish in a carnival and it went through a lot from bag to bottle and, hot to cold temperatures. It had a hard day from all the heat it had to suffer through, but I am very lucky its still alive even though its going to be the 5th year its lived.

  • Goldfish are orange.
  • Goldfish can live for many years.
  • Goldfish can become over 1 foot long in lakes.
  • Goldfish are a common treat for cats.
  • Goldfish will eat as much as you give them until they explode.
